title: Homelab

Welcome to my homelab. It started a few years ago with an outdated HP desktop and a TrueNAS
Scale install, and has since grown into a multi-node Proxmox cluster with dedicated backup
infrastructure, a rack's worth of networking gear, and more self-hosted services than I can
count. It hasn't been easy all of the time. I have brought down the family network enough times along the way that I am the first suspect in every internet outage. In the end, I have learned so much from it. It has been one of my passions that I don't get tired of. There are so many different options from building and running through cyber labs, self-hosted docker services, and experimenting with hardware. I haven't been able to pin down one specializaiton, but I think having so many options helps me to stay productive with my tendency to get bored of a topic.

coexists with the rest of the household, the tools that make it all manageable, and the
self-hosted apps running day to day.
