Welcome to my homelab. It started a few years ago with an outdated HP desktop and a TrueNAS
Scale install, and has since grown into a multi-node Proxmox cluster with dedicated backup
infrastructure, a rack's worth of networking gear, and more self-hosted services than I can
count. I have brought down the family network enough times along the way that I am the first
suspect in every internet outage.

Browse the categories below for hardware builds, infrastructure write-ups, how the lab
coexists with the rest of the household, the tools that make it all manageable, and the
self-hosted apps running day to day.
