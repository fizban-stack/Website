Talkin' About [Infosec] News is a long-running weekly show from Black Hills Information Security, streamed live on YouTube every Monday. A rotating panel of BHIS pentesters and friends works through a stack of stories gathered by their community news team — breaches, malware campaigns, exploit trends, privacy issues, and the occasional tech policy shakeup — with the kind of practical, opinionated commentary you'd expect from people who do offensive security for a living.

## Key Features

- Weekly live show, recorded and republished as a podcast
- Rotating panel of BHIS pentesters and community guests
- Community-sourced news roundup rather than a single host's picks
- Practical takes on breaches, malware, and exploit trends alongside the headlines

## Why I Recommend It

It's the fastest way I've found to keep up with what actually matters in security news without wading through the hype myself. The panel format means you get several practitioners' takes on the same story, which is more useful than a single writeup.
