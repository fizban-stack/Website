Ben Sadeghipour (NahamSec) is a leading figure in the bug bounty community. His content focuses on the "hunt"—specifically the reconnaissance phase and how to automate the discovery of vulnerabilities in large scopes.

---

## Channel Focus

### Reconnaissance
- **Asset Discovery**: Using tools like Amass, Subfinder, and httpx to map attack surfaces.
- **Content Discovery**: Finding hidden parameters and endpoints.

### Web Hacking
- **Client-Side**: XSS, CSRF, and CORS misconfigurations.
- **Server-Side**: SSRF, RCE, and SQLi in modern web stacks.

### Community
- **Interviews**: Conversations with top earners on platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd.

## Why Follow This Channel
To learn the workflows of top-tier bug bounty hunters and improve your reconnaissance methodology.
