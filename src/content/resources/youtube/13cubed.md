13Cubed is the premier channel for Digital Forensics and Incident Response (DFIR). It provides detailed technical breakdowns of how to analyze evidence, investigate compromised systems, and understand Windows internals.

---

## Channel Focus

### Forensics Artifacts
- **Windows Internals**: Deep dives into the MFT, Registry, and Event Logs.
- **Memory Forensics**: Analyzing RAM captures to find malware that never touches the disk.

### Incident Response
- **Triage**: Rapidly identifying patient zero in a network compromise.
- **Tooling**: Mastery of tools like Zimmerman's tools, KAPE, and Volatility.

## Why Follow This Channel
If you need to understand *how* an attack happened after the fact, 13Cubed is the definitive resource.
